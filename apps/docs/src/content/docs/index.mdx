## Why this exists

[`material-react-table`](https://github.com/KevinVandy/material-react-table) is one of the most complete React data tables ever shipped, but it ships MUI as a hard dependency and is no longer being maintained upstream. If your design system is shadcn/ui + Tailwind, you either pulled in MUI for one component or rebuilt a table from scratch. `shadstack-table` is the third option: a shadcn-native fork that ports every MUI primitive to its shadcn/ui equivalent and continues forward as its own library.

## What you get

- TanStack Table v8 under the hood
- shadcn/ui primitives — Tailwind v4, Radix, your own CSS variables drive theming
- Full data-table feature surface: filtering, sorting, grouping, row selection,
  pinning, editing, virtualization, detail panels
- Zero MUI in your bundle
